Description

Early life and Education

Nay Htoo Naing was born on January 1, 1979 in the city of Nengchuong. After graduating from high school, he prepared to enter the Military Academy, but failed the examination. Therefore, he went to live in Yangon from Keru River and began his artistic career.

Career

Nay Htoo Naing entered the art world in his 20s, around 1997, when he began studying the arts. His teacher was director Aung Min Thein, who also taught him acting skills to become an actor. Nay Htoo Naing's first film was "Brook". The film that made Nay Htoo Naing famous was "Nameless Sword". Nay Htoo Naing has not only directed video films but also appeared in blockbuster films. He has directed hundreds of video films. He also works as a film director. He directed and wrote the screenplay for the film "Amon Sing Amon Win Ka Pa".Nay Htoo Naing opened the Action Academy in 2018.
